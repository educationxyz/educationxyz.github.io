The Why

Education is the most important tool to increase the scope and scale of consciousness and lead humanity to a better future. Humanity would not have advanced without transfer of knowledge through generations.

Next

Classroom learning

The current classroom model of education delivery fails at scale since it is difficult to provide sufficient quality teachers and resources. In India, there are 600 million people under the age of 25 who need these resources.

Next

MOOCs

Many universities were generous to contribute. MOOCs came to the rescue. It became possible for students in the remote parts of the world to take courses by the best teachers of the world.

Next

However,

It is challenging to find the best course for your needs with so many courses available. Also, there is a need for a course independent discussion platform where people from different backgrounds can bring different perspectives to the problems.

Next

What we do

educationXYZ solves the problem of discovery and make learning a more fun and social experience with an open discussion platform that makes acquiring knowledge fun.
